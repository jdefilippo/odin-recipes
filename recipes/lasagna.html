Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the best food of all time. It is truly a classic and easy to make once you get the hang of it.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozarrella
  • Ricotta
  • Spinach
  • Basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Lay out noodles
  2. Combine cheese and spinach
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  4. Bake for 40 minutes
